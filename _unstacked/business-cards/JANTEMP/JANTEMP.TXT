January Desktop Publishing Contest
Entry submitted by Charles T. Dohrman (CDohrman@aol.com)

This template was created on MS Publisher v2.0 for use with the Canon Bubblejet 200e.

640x480x256 resolution.

This is a template for use on Publisher 1.x or 2.0.  It sets up 10 business cards on a page.  All you have to do is put the name of your business, address and phone, and 3 short phrases describing your business.  Further info is provided within the template on page 2.
