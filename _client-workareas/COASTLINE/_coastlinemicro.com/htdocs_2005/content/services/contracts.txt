<b>Time and Material Service Contract</b>
<ul type="circle">
<li>Customer pays the standard rate on as needed basis.</li>
<li>Support available Monday - Friday, 8a - 5p PST, except holidays.</li>
<li>After hours support available Monday - Friday, except holidays, by appointment only.</li>
<li>After hours support is billed at 1.5X (1.5 x rate = billable rate) of the standard rate.</li>
<li>Phone support billed in 15-minute increments. Minimum billing is 15 minutes.</li>
<li>Onsite support billed in 15-minute increments. Minimum billing is 1 hour.</li>
<li>Typical onsite response time, next day but not guaranteed. 3rd day onsite guaranteed.</li>
<li>To lock in rates customer must sign up for a Time and Material Service Contract.</li>
<li>Contract applies to Level 2 Techs and Level 3 Engineers only.</li>
<li>Level 4 Engineers are billed at the standard applicable rate.</li>
</ul>	
<br>
<b>Monthly Service Contract</b>
<ul type="circle">
<li>Customer purchases a block of hours (5 hours minimum) at a reduced rate* to be used per month.</li>
<li>Unused hours do not roll over to the following month.</li>
<li>When the customer requires technical support (onsite or phone) the hours used will be deducted from the purchased block.</li>
<li>Support available Monday - Friday, 8a - 5p PST, except holidays.</li>
<li>After hours support available Monday - Friday, except holidays, by appointment only.</li>
<li>After hours support is billed at 1.5X (1.5 x rate = billable rate) of the standard rate.</li>
<li>Phone support billed in 15-minute increments. Minimum billing is 15 minutes.</li>
<li>Onsite support billed in 15-minute increments. Minimum billing is 1 hour.</li>
<li>Level 3 Engineer will be assigned to the account.</li>
<li>Engineer will be onsite weekly at a predetermined date and time to work on any open support issues.</li>
<li>Typical onsite response time, same day but not guaranteed. Next day onsite guaranteed.</li>
<li>Contract applies to Level 2 Techs and Level 3 Engineers only.</li>
<li>Level 4 Engineers are billed at the standard applicable rate.</li>
</ul>
