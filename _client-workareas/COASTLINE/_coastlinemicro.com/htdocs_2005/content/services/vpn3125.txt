<font class="stext"><b>Coastline Micro Data Security Offering for the Service 
Provider or Large Central Site</b><img width="215" height="99" vspace="15" hspace="15" align="right" src="images/image002.jpg"><br>
Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) enable the secure transmission of private data 
over the Internet, allowing for rapid set-up and low-cost management of intranets, 
extranets, remote connections, and wireless LANs. Now you can achieve a unique 
combination of powerful, cost-effective, Internet-based business access and unparalleled 
communications security. <br>
<br>
The Coastline Micro 3125 VPN Gateway is an ideal solution for service providers 
and large central sites requiring stellar performance and high rack density. The 
Coastline Micro 3125 VPN Gateway offers full authentication, data encryption, 
hardware acceleration, routing, and firewall features, handling up to 10,000 concurrent 
tunnels and up to 85 Mbps 3DES throughput. Its intuitive interfaces make configuration 
and management exceptionally quick and easy. <br>
<br>
The Coastline Micro 3125 VPN Gateway comes complete with Coastline Micro VPN Client 
software, which enables remote users to transfer mission-critical information 
via the Internet from a desktop or laptop. The Coastline Micro VPN Client interoperates 
with other industry-standard authentication and authorization schemes to deliver 
seamless connectivity across a wide range of last-mile technologies.</font><br>
