                  <font class="stext"><br>
                  The Coastline Micro 8125 and 8120 e-Commerce Directors provide 
                  a complete combination of intelligent traffic management and 
                  encryption technology. By intelligently balancing network loads 
                  and offloading Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) processing, the Coastline 
                  Micro 8125 and 8120 e-Commerce Directors significantly reduce 
                  server overhead. IT professionals benefit from centralized load 
                  balancing and SSL management using secure a Command Line Interface 
                  (CLI) and Graphical User Interface (GUI). From transaction integrity 
                  to Intelligent Data Replication (IDR) to high-performance SSL 
                  offload, the Coastline Micro 8125 and 8120 e-Commerce Directors 
                  provide a reliable, secure end-user customer experience.
                  <p>Deliver fast, secure and reliable traffic management with 
                    Gigabit Ethernet connectivity using the Coastline Micro 8125 
                    and 8120 e-Commerce Directors. Rack-mountable and easily installed 
                    between your router and server farm, the Coastline Micro 8125 
                    and 8120 e-Commerce Directors feature intelligent management 
                    for secure transactions, and combine Layers 4-7 awareness 
                    to direct and prioritize web content.</p>
                  <p>The Coastline Micro 8125 and 8120 e-Commerce Directors offload 
                    SSL encryption/decryption with proprietary software and innovative 
                    hardware acceleration. The Coastline Micro 8125 and 8120 e-Commerce 
                    Directors also provide multiple levels of Layer 7 intelligence. 
                    The Intelligent Session Recovery feature provides the ability 
                    to &quot;look inside&quot; secure transactions, automatically 
                    detect relevant HTTP 400 or 500 errors, and transparently 
                    redirect the transaction to another available server, optimizing 
                    the customer experience. The Coastline Micro 8125 and 8120 
                    e-Commerce Directors include application-layer content verification, 
                    providing awareness of the user experience beyond simple load 
                    balancing. <br>
