                  <font class="stext"><br>
                  Boost speed and increase online transaction security with the 
                  Coastline Micro 7120 e-Commerce Accelerator. Compact and easy 
                  to install between routers and servers, the Coastline Micro 
                  7120 e-Commerce Accelerator offloads Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) 
                  encryption/decryption, freeing server resources and providing 
                  up to 2,200 SSL connections per second processing. As both clear-text 
                  and secure traffic increases, the performance and Gigabit (10/100/1000) 
                  connectivity of the Coastline Micro 7120 e-Commerce Accelerator 
                  is ready to meet growing SSL traffic needs. 
                  <p>With its enhanced scalability features, the Coastline Micro 
                    7120 e-Commerce Accelerator can be deployed in a variety of 
                    modes to accommodate peak traffic periods easily and economically. 
                    In cascade mode, excess SSL traffic can be transferred to 
                    the next unit or to the server farm, providing the ability 
                    to scale beyond 8,000 SSL connections per second without the 
                    introduction of a load-balancing device. Failover/pass-through 
                    technology helps reduce single point of failure concerns so 
                    that your e-Commerce business remains quick, reliable and 
                    secure.</p>
                  <p>The Coastline Micro 7120 e-Commerce Accelerator can be managed 
                    remotely via telnet, SSH, or SNMP through the Out-of-Band 
                    management port or directly through the serial console port. 
                    Event monitoring provides monitoring of key accelerator metrics 
                    like SSL connections per second (cps), as well as alerts for 
                    events such as encryption status change, refused SSL connections, 
                    threshold alerts, overload alerts, and network link status. 
                    Health monitoring alerts are also available to enable product 
                    reliability and uptime.<br>
                  </p></font>
