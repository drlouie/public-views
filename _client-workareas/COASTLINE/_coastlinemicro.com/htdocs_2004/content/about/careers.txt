JOB TITLE: Driver
DEPARTMENT: Shipping and Receiving
POSITION TYPE: Hourly, Full Time

JOB TITLE: Outside Sales Consultant
DEPARTMENT: Sales
POSITION TYPE: Base + Commission, Full Time

PRIMARY FUNCTION: 
o	Develop new accounts
o	Create new business with existing accounts
o	Attain and maintain a pipeline of qualified leads via prospecting, 
	premise visits, telemarketing, customer referrals and networking in 
	order to generate profitable revenue.
o	Generate appointments with the primary decision maker of prospective 
	customers and existing customers.
o	Develop proposals based on prospect or existing customer requirements.
o	Respond to RFP's.
o	Provide technical solutions, conducting presentations, and 
	demonstrations for prospective clients.
o	Interface with each of the inside sales consultants to make sure that 
	they are effectively managing their accounts to the level that the 
	customer requires.
o	Assist with customer quotations and other related sales functions as required

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS, KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS:
o	3 to 5 year of experience in the computer industry
o	Proficient at the technical level with computer hardware
o	Ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing
o	Self motivated and focused, with goal-setting mentality
o	Must have proven track record and existing client base 
o	Ability to build strong business relationships with new and existing contacts
