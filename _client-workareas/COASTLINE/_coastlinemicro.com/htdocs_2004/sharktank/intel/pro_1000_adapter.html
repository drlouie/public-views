Intel® Pro/1000 T Server Adapter (RJ45)  
 
The Intel® Pro/1000 T Server Adapter is your best choice for easy, cost-effective migration to Gigabit Ethernet over Category-5 cabling. Its capabilities include high network performance and flexibility at 10/100/1000Mbps. The Intel® Pro/1000 T Server Adapter helps in lowering network support costs by utilizing the latest in advanced management technologies. Featuring the third-generation Intel 82544 Gigabit Controller, this adapter preserves your current Cat-5 network investment and gets you up to speed for today's most demanding networking needs.
