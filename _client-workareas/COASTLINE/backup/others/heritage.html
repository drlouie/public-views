Heritage
 
Since 1989, Coastline Micro has been assembling quality, built-to-order computer systems, including servers and PCs, for VARs and resellers nationwide. In that time, we have built a reputation among our loyal customer base as a company that values integrity, quality and working relationships. We have continued to expand our selection of built-to-order and pre-built products, as well as our scope of services. Today, Coastline Micro is a leading provider of end-to-end, high-quality, cost-effective computing and networking solutions.

We understand that our success is measured by the success of the VARs with whom we work. By making sure that your customers' total computing requirements are met, we help you maintain profitable, long-term relationships and increase sales. We make it a point to respond promptly to both your needs and the needs of your customers. Every member of the Coastline Micro team remains unified in accomplishing our corporate mission: to improve your bottom line by quickly, efficiently and cost-effectively delivering comprehensive computing solutions.
