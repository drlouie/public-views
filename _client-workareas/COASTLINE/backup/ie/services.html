Requirements Analysis:
Our seasoned networking specialists are available to meet with your customers' IT departments and managers, executive management, and end-users to clearly define objectives and assess the role IT plays in their business operations. Additionally, we submit reports detailing the conclusions of our analysis.


Networking:
Our technicians and engineers can provide your customers with comprehensive design, integration, management and support services. Whether implementing new networks or expanding existing networks, we ensure that the infrastructures we design are reliable, fully scalable and cost-effective. We also provide detailed documentation for enhanced ease-of-maintenance.

Configuration/Assembly:
Our highly trained technicians pre-assemble and configure your customers' choice of CPUs, upgrades, and software to their exact specifications at our in-house, leading-edge facilities. This allows you to remain focused on your core business, while ensuring that the systems we build are ready to be delivered right when your customers need them.

Testing:
Our objective is to keep product returns to a bare minimum. To achieve this, we first test all our system components for reliability, compatibility and performance. We then configure and test the assembled system to ensure peak performance before the system is installed in your customers' live work environment.


Shipping:
While we hand-deliver systems whenever possible, we also offer worldwide shipping. In addition, we can often process orders quickly to permit same-day shipping.


Internet/Intranet:
We assist your customers in building, implementing and securing robust, high-speed Internet connections, with technical support to keep their businesses continually online. Our Internet solutions include ISDN, T1, and DSL lines, e-mail applications, and Intranet/Extranet architectures that allow companies to share and manage information across their enterprises.


Technical Support:
We free your time to make more sales, by providing troubleshooting services and pre/post-sales support, both remote and onsite. Extended service contracts and onsite warranties are also available.
