File Upload Checklist:

FONTS. Please include all fonts that are used in your document. Include only those fonts that are actually used (as opposed to copying an entire font folder). Please include both parts for each font, i.e., a printer font and a screen font.

Note: Stylized fonts or multiple master fonts may cause problems with your file(s).

IMAGES. Include all images that are placed in your document. JPEG, TIFF and EPS formats are acceptable.

RESOLUTION. All images should be 300 dpi at the size of output. If your images are of lower resolution, please call and discuss this with your
Navigator/Scantech Account Representative.

COLOR. Images should be scanned CMYK; however, Navigator can convert RGB images IF the ICC profile is provided. Color outside the gamut will be handled according to perceptual rendering intent.

HAIRLINES. Hairlines must have an assigned numerical value (i.e., do not select "hairline" from the line weight menu).

PROOFREAD. Navigator is not responsible for incorrect copy supplied by clients. Please proofread your files.
