theTitle=XEROX - The Power of Services&theDescription=Xerox's Services Division wanted an easily accessible electronic sales kit for their worldwide sales force. A sales kit that packaged computer-based training CDs, a sales presentation tool CD, promotional stickers, banners, posters, and presentation folders. The CBT CD provides Xerox sales staff with a narrative animated module on each service and how to sell it. It also provided electronic collateral material.
