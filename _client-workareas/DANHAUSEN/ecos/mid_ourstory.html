Let There Be Light(Site)!

The original idea for LightSite came from Chris Calwell, one of the founders of Ecos Consulting and a leader in efforts over the last decade to improve residential lighting energy efficiency. The Ecos Consulting team now manages energy-saving residential lighting programs on behalf of utilities in six states. Ecos established a partnership with Energy Federation Inc., one of the nation's largest and best-known fulfillment houses for energy saving products, to bring LightSite to market in 1998.

We hope that LightSite is useful to you and welcome your comments and suggestions. If you have a question for Chris or the rest of the LightSite team, please email us. We'll be happy to respond as soon as possible.

Welcome to LightSite!


The Brains Behind The Bright Idea.
Chris Calwell
Chris Calwell built the first compact fluorescent torchiere prototype in 1994, and has been working to solve the halogen torchiere problem ever since. He serves as the President of Ecos Consulting, where he supervises research on residential lighting, jointly manages LightSite with Mark Newton, writes about various environmental topics, and takes a month off each year to teach college courses about energy and ecology. Chris also works as a freelance environmental photographer, plays racquetball, and tinkers with his passive solar home in Durango, CO in his (increasingly infrequent) spare time.

Mark Newton
Mark Newton works on marketing and management issues related to the LightSite as part of the Ecos Consulting team. Mark is excited to help develop and manage profitable programs for companies and consumers that solve energy efficiency and environmental challenges. When he isn't working, Mark enjoys mountain biking, road riding, cross riding and snowboarding. Feel free to drop him an e-line if you have questions about the site, feedback and suggestions, or if you just want to will your estate over to him.
