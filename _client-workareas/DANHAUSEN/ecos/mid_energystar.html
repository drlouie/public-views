ENERGY STAR® is a joint labeling effort of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and the U.S. Department of Energy to help consumers identify energy saving products. ENERGY STAR® labels appear on many products, including computers, printers, office equipment, appliances, light fixtures, light bulbs and homes. ENERGY STAR® products help prevent pollution by reducing the burning of fossil fuels to produce electricity. To learn more about the ENERGY STAR® program, visit the ENERGY STAR® web site.



The ENERGY STAR® residential light fixture program began in March of 1997. These fixtures do not use a standard threaded socket for light bulbs, but instead include a built-in ballast, which is an electronic circuit that operates a matching fluorescent lamp. All light fixtures bearing the ENERGY STAR® label meet strict federal specifications for energy efficiency, lighting quality, safety and reliability. ENERGY STAR® torchieres solve the safety problem of halogen torchieres right at its source--with compact fluorescent lighting technology. Using only 50 to 80 watts, they provide a similar amount of crisp, white light as a 300-watt halogen model. They're safer precisely because they don't waste energy creating so much heat.

Best of all, ENERGY STAR® labeled torchieres are great for the environment--by using less energy, they reduce air pollution from power plants. In fact, you'd have to cut your driving by about 500 miles each year in order to reduce carbon dioxide emissions as much as one ENERGY STAR® labeled torchiere can.

Last but not least, they're convenient. Their super-efficient bulbs only need to be replaced every four to eight years. You don't need to handle them with gloves. You don't need to wipe off your fingerprints with alcohol. You don't need to find a screwdriver to install a glass cover over them to protect yourself from ultraviolet radiation and shattered bulb fragments. And they don't need an ugly metal cage to keep the bulb away from flammable materials. Does that sound better than halogen? It is!
