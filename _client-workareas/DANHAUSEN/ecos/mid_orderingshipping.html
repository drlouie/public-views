Ordering And Shipping Information
We offer several ways to place orders with LightSite. The final order information that you supply (such as credit card information or email address) resides on our Secure Server.

Submit your order and payment information electronically:
Use our secure server to select the products you'd like to purchase from our online catalog. When you are finished shopping, simply click on the Checkout Stand button and provide us with your shipping, billing, and payment information. When prompted to provide payment information, select the Credit Card option. We accept only Visa and Master Card. Sorry, we do not currently accept American Express, Diner's Club, Player's Club, Club Med, or Culture Club.

Email us your order:
Use our secure server to select the products you'd like to purchase from our online catalog. When you are finished shopping, simply click on the Checkout Stand button. When prompted to provide payment information, select the Email order option. Your order will be emailed to us, and one of our customer service representatives will call you requesting your payment information.

Fax your order to us:
Use our secure server to select the products you'd like to purchase from our online catalog. When you are finished shopping, click on the Checkout Stand button. When prompted for Contact and Payment Information, simply print out the page. Complete the order form clearly in dark ink and fax it to us at (888) 655-6767. A customer service representative will contact you if we need any additional information or if your handwriting is as bad as that of LightSite's founder.

Order by phone:
Give us a call at: (800) 379-4121. You can call us 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to place your order with us over the phone.

Shipping Information
We ship most orders within 3 business days of receiving your order. We will contact you if any of the products you order are on backorder. Given the weight of torchieres, we have found that it is most cost effective to ship orders via UPS Ground delivery. Unfortunately we cannot ship orders to Post Office Boxes (can you imagine trying to stuff a torchiere into a post office box?)
