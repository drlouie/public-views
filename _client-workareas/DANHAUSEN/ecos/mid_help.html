At LightSite.net, your privacy matters. We created our LightSite.net privacy statement to demonstrate our firm commitment to protecting our customers' privacy.

LightSite collects and processes information for specified, legitimate purposes. We have not and will not give away, trade, or sell information obtained from our customers or site visitors. The following discloses our processes for gathering and disseminating information for LightSite.

We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server, and to administer our Web site. IP addresses are used in aggregate form for traffic analysis.

Our site's registration form requires users to submit contact information (like their name and email address). We use customer contact information from the registration form to send the user information about our company. If you would specifically prefer not to receive any follow-up mailings or email contact from us after your site visit, see the Choice/Opt-Out section below.

LightSite contains links to other sites. LightSite is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those sites.

LightSite is owned by Ecos Consulting, Inc. (www.ecosconsulting.com), which stores and protects all information provided by customers. We conduct a number of energy-saving lighting programs on behalf of electric utilities in California, Oregon, Washington, Idaho, Montana, Wisconsin and Illinois. Our contracts with those utilities often require us to keep customer information confidential, only sharing it with utility program managers and the survey teams who verify our program results.

Our site uses an order form for customers to request information, products, and services. We collect visitors' contact information (their email address) and financial information (their account or credit card numbers). Contact information from the order form is used to send orders to our customers, and financial information is used, of course, to bill customers for purchases.

In the interest of improving customer service, we occasionally survey a fraction of users about their site experience. If you tell us you don't wish to receive such inquiries, we won't send them to you.

From time to time, we may run contests or promotions on our site in which we ask visitors for contact information (like their email address). We use contact data from our contests to send users information about our company. We also use this contact data, such as email address, to notify our contest winners.

Security
This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We use VeriSign (hotlinked to http://www.verisign.com) Secure Site Services. Our VeriSign Secure Server ID works in conjunction with Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology, which is the standard protocol for secure, Web-based communications. VeriSign's Seal was voted the #1 sign of trust on the Internet (Cheskin/Studio Archetype Study). We use Secure Socket Layers at the highest possible encryption strength that customers' browsers are capable of handling. In addition, financial information is protected by our ISP's network procedures. All LightSite.net employees are aware of these security procedures (via internal education) and are periodically briefed on any new security considerations.
