It takes courage to begin the journey in recovery. It also takes courage to reach out a hand to those struggling with their dependencies. You can help.


Equally courageous in their quest for "responsible recovery" are the dedicated individuals and community groups who support NDFW. Our residential center remains under 24-hour supervision by a dedicated, trained staff, including a medical director, a nurse practitioner, and a clinical director who is a licensed marriage and family therapist. Following in the footsteps of our founders are NDFW's volunteer board of directors, supported by Las Socias, the Decorative Arts Society and proceeds from our thrift store Repeat Boutique as well as special events. We welcome contributions from individuals, business and community groups. Contact NDFW today to ask how you can help us help women begin their journey in recovery. (800)93-WOMEN.
