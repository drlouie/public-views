Over 20 years ago, our founders set a course for our unique treatment and recovery center. We've been helping women find New Directions ever since.


Founded in 1977 by the Junior League of Orange County, New Directions for Women is a not-for-profit residential treatment and recovery facility dedicated to helping women with alcohol and drug dependencies. Originally established in response to a shortage of affordable rehabilitation beds for women experiencing problems with alcoholism, today NDFW treats chemical dependencies in a 30-bed residential setting located in Costa Mesa, near Newport Beach's Back Bay and the Nature Preserve. Both state licensed and certified, our award-winning facility offers an extraordinarily high recovery rate. We attribute this success to our woman-focused mission and individualized recovery programs.
