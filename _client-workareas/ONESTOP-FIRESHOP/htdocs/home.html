One Stop Fire Shop was established to satisfy the growing needs of the fire fighting industry. Having years of experience working with fire departments around the world, the staff at One Stop Fire Shop has a deep understanding of the needs of the fire fighting professional. That experience is a valuable asset to your department when it comes to the rapid, economical procurement of most any emergency fire equipment.

One Stop Fire Shop offers unique packages of equipment making the purchasing process more convenient and more economical. One Stop Fire Shop’s mission is to offer a one-stop source for all rescue and fire fighting needs.

One Stop Fire Shop is a woman owned small business.
